Creative Writing Exercise: "The Character Through the Mirror"
Published on March 22, 2025

If you're struggling with writing because your characters feel unclear, distant, or simply underdeveloped, this exercise is designed to help you bridge the gap between you and your characters. It will support you in continuing your work and freeing yourself from writer's block. The goal of this exercise is to help you build a fully rounded character by exploring their appearance, inner thoughts, and how they interact with the world.
