Creative Writing Exercise: "The Character Through the Mirror"

Published on March 22, 2025

If you're struggling with writing because your characters feel unclear, distant, or simply because you haven't explored their depths as you should, this exercise is designed to help you bridge the distance between you and your characters. It will support your writing process and help you overcome writer's block.

The aim of this exercise is to help you build a fully developed character by exploring their appearance, inner thoughts, and how they interact with the world.
