About Us

Home / About Us

Al Jazeera Library is a multifaceted cultural center based in the UAE, consisting of a library, a publishing house, a creative writing workshop, and a cultural platform. Al Jazeera Library positions itself as one of the driving forces of soft power in the Arab world, striving through questions, words, and ideas to contribute to a societal cultural transformation towards the values of freedom, pluralism, acceptance of others, and fundamental rights for all.

Vision:

To be the primary destination for reading enthusiasts and knowledge seekers in the Arab world by providing a comprehensive and user-friendly electronic library, offering the best printed and electronic books at the highest quality and best prices.

Mission:

We aim to promote a reading culture and facilitate access to knowledge online by offering a distinguished e-commerce experience, reliable customer service, and a wide range of books in various fields to meet the interests of all readers.

Goals:

Expand the user base locally and across the Arab world by offering reliable and suitable services for all categories.
Provide a diverse collection of books covering literature, sciences, self-development, school and university textbooks, and more.
Support local authors and publishers by providing a platform to showcase their works and reach a broader audience.
Enhance user experience by improving the website interface, search options, secure payment methods, and delivery services.
Promote a reading culture through awareness campaigns, seasonal discounts, and motivational content on social media platforms.
