Yoga with Cats (Available)

Maria Van Bruggen

There are days when we wish to read a special book — a book that not only tells us which paths to take, but also makes us feel better about our bodies, and even brings a smile to our faces. "Yoga with Cats" is that kind of book. It helps inspire your daily workouts through short, quick messages at the end of each session. This book includes 32 exercises, each showing a yoga pose with the unique involvement of your cat to help you perform the exercises in the best way possible.
